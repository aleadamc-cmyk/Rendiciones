ARCHIVO 3: references/logo-design-layout.md # HGT Logo, Design Principle & Layout Reference ## Logo ### Components- **Globe icon** (emblem): Crosshair-style globe in orange outline- **Wordmark**: "Hanseatic Global" in white/dark, "Terminals" in HGT Orange bold ### Logo Versions| Version | Background | When to use ||---|---|---|| Full color (white + orange) | Dark / HGT Greyblue | Preferred version || Full color (dark + orange) | White / light | Standard light background || Monochrome | Any dark background | 3D applications, signage, special cases | ### Logo Layout- Icon always to the LEFT of wordmark- "Hanseatic Global" on first line (regular weight)- "Terminals" on second line (bold, HGT Orange)- Horizontal divider line between icon and text block (orange) ### Logo Sizing Rules (signage)- Wordmark width = 80% of medium width- Spacing from edge = 10% (0.1x) of medium width ### Forbidden- Do NOT stretch, rotate, or recolor the logo- Do NOT use orange background behind logo- Do NOT use gradient on logo elements- Do NOT separate icon from wordmark (except app icons) --- ## Design Element: The Emblem (Globe) The globe/emblem is used as a large-scale decorative background element. ### Rules for emblem as design element- **Size**: Cover at least 50–80% of the longer side of the format- **Line weight**: 2× the wordmark's stroke weight (general rule)- **For large-scale**: 1.5× wordmark stroke (recommended for signage/large formats)- **Color**: White lines on dark backgrounds; orange accent arc only- **Position**: Typically bottom-left, bottom-right, or cropped at corner- Use as **outline only** — never filled ### Orientation VariantsThe globe can appear cropped from any corner/edge, showing partial sphere with:- White grid lines- Single orange arc (the outer circle highlight)- Orange crosshair lines (+) --- ## The Line A key brand design element — a horizontal orange line used as:- Separator between headline and content- Visual anchor/divider in layouts- Motion element (appears to "exit" from the crosshair) **Usage**: Always in HGT Orange. Thickness proportional to context. --- ## PowerPoint Slide Layouts ### Cover Slides (Title)- **Dark version**: HGT Greyblue background + globe emblem (white/orange) in bottom-left corner- **Photo version**: Full-bleed photo with dark overlay + globe emblem top-left- Logo: top-right corner- Title: center-left, white bold- Subtitle + date: lighter weight below title- Footer note (e.g. "For intern use only"): bottom-right in HGT Orange ### Section Divider Slides- Dark background (HGT Greyblue)- Globe emblem: right side, large (cropped)- Section number + title: left, bold white- Orange underline below title ### Content Slides (light)- White/light grey background- Page title: top, black bold (above colored band)- Content area: light grey band- Headline 1 with orange underline- Logo: bottom-right, small- Page number: bottom-left- Two-column layout option available ### Content Slides (dark)- HGT Greyblue background- Same structure as light, but all text white- Orange accents on headlines and bullets ### Text + Image Split Slide- Left half: dark (HGT Greyblue) with large text- Right half: full-bleed photo- Logo: top-right on dark area ### Chart Slides- Light grey background- Two chart panels side by side- Orange headline underline separator- Charts use brand color sequence (see colors-typography.md) --- ##
